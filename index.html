Who we are

AIConnect is a graduation project developed by Computer Science students at Jordan University of Science and Technology (JUST). The project was created by students who understand both academic learning and industry expectations, and aim to help students present their capabilities more effectively in real opportunities.

AIConnect analyzes student CVs, highlights key strengths, and recommends relevant job opportunities. At the same time, it helps companies identify suitable candidates based on their requirements. Our goal is to support students in showcasing their value clearly and to help companies connect with qualified candidates in a smarter and more efficient way.

Meet the Team

What is AIConnect?

AIConnect connects IT students and companies through smarter matching. Students upload their CVs to receive relevant opportunities, and companies can reach the right candidates faster.

For IT students

Upload your CV, let the platform analyze it, and receive tailored internship and job suggestions that match your background and interests.

For companies

Create job offers and instantly view students whose profiles align with your requirements, helping you shortlist candidates efficiently.

Powered by AI

The system extracts key information from CVs and job descriptions and computes a matching score to recommend the most relevant opportunities.

Our goals

From learning to real impact

Help IT students apply their knowledge by connecting them with real-world internships and job opportunities.

Save time for companies

Allow HR teams to focus on interviewing the right candidates – not reading hundreds of CVs manually.

Increase fair opportunities

Provide students from different universities and backgrounds with equal access to high-quality opportunities based on their capabilities.

How it works

1
Sign up

Students and companies create their accounts to access the AIConnect platform.

2
AI analysis

The platform analyzes student CVs and company job descriptions to understand technologies, experience, and requirements.

3
Smart matching

Students receive recommended offers, and companies see relevant candidates based on a matching score – both sides can accept or reject through the platform.
